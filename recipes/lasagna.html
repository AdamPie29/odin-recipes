Chicken Taco Lasagna Recipe

Description

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna.

What happens when you take an Italian classic, and combine the sweet flavours of Mexico? You get the best of both worlds, with a lasagna that is both parts savoury and sweet. Perfect for your next family gathering or Super Bowl party.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles
