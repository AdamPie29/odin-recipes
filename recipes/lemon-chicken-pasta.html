Lemon Chicken Pasta Recipe

Description

Debra Barone's famous lemon chicken over pasta

Ever wanted to have the famous Everybody Loves Raymondlemon chicken? Well how about that same lemon chicken over delicious pasta? Perfect for every occasion. Impress your wife!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package linguine pasta
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (8 ounce) package button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, cubed and softened
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
